* Insertion Sort
- This is the basic version of insertion sort.
- Worst time: $\Theta(n^2)$ 
#+BEGIN_SRC clojure :results output
  (defn ins-sort [arr]
    (reduce (fn [res inp]
              (let [[less more] (split-with #(< % inp) res)]
                (concat less [inp] more)))
            []
            arr))
